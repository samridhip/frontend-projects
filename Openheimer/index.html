Julius Robert Openheimer

I am become Death,the destroyer of worlds

  • 1904 born in New York City
  • 1925 graduated from harvard
  • 1927 Doctor of Philosophy at the age of 23
  • 1927 was awarded a United Ststes National Research Council fellowship to the Caltech
  • 1928 gave lectures at University of Leiden, The Netherlands
  • 1931 co-write a paper on "Relativistic Theory of the Photoelectric Effects
  • 1942 headed Manhattan Project
  • 1945 performed trinity test near Alamogordo, New Mexico
  • 1946 was awarded the Medal for Merit
  • 1947 accepted directorship of the Institute for Advanced Study in Princeton, New Jersey
  • 1947 appointed as the chairman of nuclear research and weapons issues
  • 1954 was subjected to security hearing which resulted into his Q clearance being revoked
  • 1957 France made him an Officer of the Legion of Honor
  • 1962 was elected a Foreign Member of the Royal Society in Britain
  • 1967 fell into coma and died in Princeton,New Jersey
